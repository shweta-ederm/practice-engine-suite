Brand identity: Practice Engine

Direction: Evergreen and Ink

The umbrella brand keeps the wordmark's green, refined into a deeper evergreen for contrast and pairing with an ink neutral. Products own the vivid accents; the platform stays calm, grounded, and premium. Three approved lockup variations:

PRACTICE ENGINE
A. Heritage Sage
The original logo green (#6CAC72) with ink. For print and legacy contexts.
PRACTICE ENGINE
B. Evergreen Ink Primary
Evergreen #3E8E5C on ink. The default digital identity, used across the suite.
PRACTICE ENGINE
C. Product Spectrum
Ink wordmark with the five product accents as the rule. For suite-level marketing moments.
Usage. B is the working identity everywhere in product. A is reserved for contexts that must match the original logo exactly. C appears only at suite level, never inside a single product.

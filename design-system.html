Brand identity: the Product Spectrum logo

One logo, many sizes

Practice Engine has a single logo: PRACTICE in the original logo green over the product spectrum rule, with ENGINE in ink. The spectrum carries one stripe per product, so the mark grows with the suite. Use the size that fits the context; never restyle or recolor the parts.

PE monogram over the spectrum on ink: 80, 48, and 32px. At 16px the monogram drops and the spectrum alone remains.
Rules. PRACTICE is always logo green (#6CAC72), ENGINE is always ink, and the spectrum rule always lists the product colors in suite order. The spectrum is reserved for the logo; never reuse it as a decorative element inside a product.

Stat card variants

Pick by prominence, not preference
KPI banner
1,284
Edge stat $48,320 supporting caption
Edge stat 4.2x supporting caption

Two tiers on dashboards. The edge stat (`.stat-edge`, color via `--edge`, cycling through the active chart tokens) is the standard for every app's metric rows; stat cards carry no icons. The KPI banner (`.kpi-card`, color via `--kpi`) is reserved for a page's two or three headline numbers. The plain borderless stat card remains only for neutral, non-dashboard contexts such as settings summaries. Do not mix tiers within the same row.
